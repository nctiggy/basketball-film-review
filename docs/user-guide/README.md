# Basketball Film Review - User Guide

Welcome to the Basketball Film Review application! This guide will help you get started with the platform.

## Overview

Basketball Film Review is a web application designed to help coaches share game film and statistics with their players and parents. The platform allows coaches to:

- Upload and manage game videos
- Create clips with timestamps and annotations
- Assign clips to specific players
- Track player statistics across games
- Share content securely with players and parents

## User Roles

The application has three distinct user roles, each with different capabilities:

### Coach
Coaches have full access to create and manage content. They can create teams, upload videos, create clips, assign them to players, and track statistics.

[Read the Coach Guide](coach-guide.md)

### Player
Players have read-only access to clips and stats assigned specifically to them. They can view clips, acknowledge they've watched them, and track their personal statistics.

[Read the Player Guide](player-guide.md)

### Parent
Parents can view all content assigned to their linked children, providing a way to stay involved in their child's basketball development.

[Read the Parent Guide](parent-guide.md)

## Getting Started

1. **Coaches**: Sign in with your Google account to get started immediately
2. **Players**: Use the invite link provided by your coach to create your account
3. **Parents**: Use the parent invite link provided by your coach to link to your child's account

## Need Help?

- Check the appropriate guide for your role above
- Contact your coach or system administrator for support
- Report technical issues to your IT department
